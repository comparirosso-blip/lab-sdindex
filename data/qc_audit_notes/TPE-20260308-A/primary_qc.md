2. **`Vg_subtype = geometric_organic` is weakly supported (minor).** The record describes curves and multi-level atrium structure but attributes these to architectural design, not organic form. 「曲線欄杆」is manufactured geometry, not organic geometry. The word "organic" does not appear in any field note. A subtype like `geometric_architectural` or `geometric_curvilinear` would align more tightly with the evidence. Low-stakes given the low score, but affects subtype consistency across the dataset.

3. **`Ve_trigger = self_time_projection` does useful work but is under-documented (minor).** The Ve note states: 「時間感來自自我投射——想像自己在別處——而非空間本身產生的時間重量」. This is a clear and interesting trigger — the observer's emotional content is generated by escape fantasy, not by the site. But the note does not describe what the projection was toward (Kyoto? home? an ideal space?), which limits the trigger's reusability as a comparative data point.
